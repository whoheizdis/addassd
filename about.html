About Me

Helena Marie B. Samson

Hello there! You can call me Helena! I am someone who finds meaning in emotions, stories, and moments that often go unnoticed. I’m interested in videogames, music, writing, and self-reflection, especially how feelings like growth, love, uncertainty, and healing shape who we become. I created this blog as a space to express those thoughts honestly—to turn experiences and inspirations into words, and to share reflections that others might relate to. This blog is not about having everything figured out, but about documenting the journey of becoming, one post at a time.

Fun Fact About Me #1: Whenever I see a name of someone I know as the main character of the book, I either try to not think about that person or not read the book because it icked me that bad.
