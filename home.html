Welcome


Welcome to Between Deadlines and Daydreams! This is a little corner of the internet where I share thoughts, reflections, and stories from the busy life of a student. Here, the chaos of assignments, exams, and deadlines meets quiet moments of imagination, creativity, and daydreaming. Whether it’s a small insight from school, a personal reflection, or a creative idea I couldn’t keep to myself, this blog is a space to pause, think, and explore what lies in between the rush of everyday tasks.

I hope this blog can be a place where you find something familiar, inspiring, or even a little comforting. Life as a student can be hectic, but it’s also full of small lessons, unexpected joys, and little moments worth noticing. Through these posts, I want to share the highs and lows, the struggles and discoveries, and the little stories that often go untold. So take a moment, read through, and maybe even daydream a little — because sometimes, the best ideas happen in between deadlines.
